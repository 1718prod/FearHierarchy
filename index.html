Director's Vision

"Fear Hierarchy was brought to me by Matthew to direct and immediately I was interested... Her being frozen in time means something to me. To remember every awful moment you want to forget. Healing is always possible but only after you face your demons."

— TINA REEVES, DIRECTOR

The Story

To overcome a fear of the dark before marrying her fiancé, a woman must confront the darker truth behind her phobia, before it destroys their relationship.

Fear Hierarchy explores the psychological landscape where trauma manifests as physical paralysis, and the journey toward healing requires facing the memories we've buried deepest.
